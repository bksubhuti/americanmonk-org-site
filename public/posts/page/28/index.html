Razors and Switchblades

Razors and Switchblades These are the types of razors that most monks use in Myanmar. I used one of these for about eight years with one of the sides modified so it did not having the safety bar. We called the sides; “safety side” and “dangerous side.” With the dangerous side, one can wait a week or even 4 weeks before shaving. The safety bar is what causes the blade to clog and the “dangerous side” removes “clogging” from the formula.
